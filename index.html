Royal Metal IND. CO., LTD

ROYAL Metal Industrial Co., Ltd. is a Korean precision metalworking company established in the 1950s, specialising in nail clippers and nose hair trimmers. Based on traditional craftsmanship and continuous innovation, we export to 5 continents worldwide and develop world-class nose hair trimmers in-house. ROYAL grows as a trusted brand, always putting people first through technology.
